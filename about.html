About

Traveling is an independent services company that offers you the opportunity to create your dream trip, particularly in the international. We aim to support your family, organisation or company in the development of your traveling project in France and abroad. We work with local authorities, companies and start-ups on all aspects of traveling.
Planning a vacation doesn’t have to be a pain. That’s why we want to show you a great system you can use to plan the perfect trip.
