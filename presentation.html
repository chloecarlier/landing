Presentation

Planning a trip can feel like both the most exciting AND the most overwhelming part of travel. Sure, creating a Pinterest board full of gorgeous pictures is fun, but then you start trying to figure out the actual logistics and it hits you … oh god, this is SO MUCH WORK . Cue panic attacks.

But planning a trip doesn’t have to be the source of constant anxiety and overwhelm! Through the years, we’ve perfected our trip PLANNING PROCESS, and we’ve got it down to a fine science. Well, maybe not a science, but sort of like a general step-by-step outline. Breaking down each of the steps to planning a trip helps me cut through the confusion and tackle stuff in a practical, down to earth way. Plus, it makes you feel like the most organized person on the planet, which is VERY good for your ego.

At the end of our planning process, we end up with a BIG DOCUMENT containing all the information you need for your trip: everything from where we are staying and how to get there, to what to eat. You take that document with you and REFER to it constantly during your trip.

We are going to teach you ALL our travel planning tips, and by the end of this massive guide, you’ll be ITCHING to plan some fabulous vacation.
